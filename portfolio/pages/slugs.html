Nudibranch Stickers

these paintings were a result of my self-initiated September Slug Sticker Challenge. my goal was to create one nudibranch painting that was worthy of being printed as a sticker every day. unfortunately, school got in the way and I ended with fewer and worse quality paintings. these are some of my favorites.
